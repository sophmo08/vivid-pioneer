I (co-)supervise Bachelor's and Master's theses on workplace inequality across disciplines such as Public Administration and Management, Psychology, and Data Science. I guide students in developing strong theoretical frameworks and applying rigorous quantitative methods to explore topics like diversity, discrimination, leadership, and organizational change. My supervision spans students from the Politics, Public Administration and Management track, as well as those studying Psychology or Data Science.

**Level:** Undergraduate & Graduate | **Institution:** University of Konstanz | **Since:** 2022
